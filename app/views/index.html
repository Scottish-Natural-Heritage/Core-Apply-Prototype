Licensing

Applying for a licence

Before applying to us for a licence, read our general information about the licensing process.

Explore our A–Z guide to find out about licensing for a specific protected species, including how to apply for each type of licence.

We advise that you read the relevant standing advice relating to planning and development if applicable.

We may need to pass your application on to relevant partner agencies who may contact you to discuss or arrange a site visit.

Renewing a licence

You must apply for a new licence annually for a site, rather than request a renewal. You must have submitted a return for your previous licence before we will consider issuing a new one.

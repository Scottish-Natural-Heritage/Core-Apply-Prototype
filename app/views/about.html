About the licence holder

{% if data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Prevent serious damage' %}

The licence holder needs to be the person who is experiencing the damage to livestock or their feed. Typically they will be the owner or tenant of the farm.

{% elseif data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Public health and safety' %}

The licence holder needs to be the person who has the authority to make changes to the fabric of the building, such as installing spikes or nets. Typically they will be the owner, tenant or property manager for the site.

{% elseif data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Air safety' %}

The licence holder needs to be the person who is experiencing the air safety problem.

{% endif %}

Applying on behalf of someone else, for example: land agents or factors

If the licence holder is your client or tenant you can apply for a licence on their behalf. If so you must ask for their permission to:

  • apply on their behalf; and
  • provide their personal details

After you have completed the application we'll email them.

If they confirm that they will be the licence holder, we'll process the application.

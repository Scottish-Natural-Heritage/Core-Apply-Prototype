Before you start

{% if data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Prevent serious damage' %}

You will require a prevent serious damage licence if you need to control wild birds to prevent serious damage to livestock, foodstuffs for livestock, crops, vegetables, fruit, growing timber, fisheries or inland waters. ​

To complete this application we will ask you about:​

  • the species and numbers causing damage
  • the serious damage being caused
  • the location of where damage is occurring
  • when damage is occurring
  • what preventative measures you have tried or intend to try

You may need to upload a map or diagram showing where the damage is taking place or other documentation in support of your application.

We advise you to read the relevant section of our website for further details of what information is required for the type of licence you are applying for.

{% elseif data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Public health and safety' %}

You will require a public health and safety licence where wild birds pose a threat to public health or safety and there is no satisfactory alternative, we may issue a licence to permit certain actions that might otherwise constitute an offence. ​

To complete this application we will ask you about:​

  • the specific health or safety issue
  • what preventative measures you have tried and intend to try
  • the licensed activities you are planning

Depending on which licensed activities you select, we will ask you about:

  • nest or egg destruction - an estimated number of nests
  • relocating chicks or lethal control - exact numbers
{% elseif data['isLicenceRequired'] == 'Air safety' %}

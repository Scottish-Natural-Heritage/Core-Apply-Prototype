Before you start

To complete this application we will ask you about:​

  • the species of wild bird
  • the serious damage being caused
  • what preventative measures you have tried or intend
  • the location where the damage is occurring

If wild birds are causing damage to livestock you may need to upload a map or diagram showing where the damage is taking place.
